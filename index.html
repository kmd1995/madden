Tournament rules

Rules must be followed if your competing in the tournament.

  • 21 point rule in effect (21 at any point in game, person down gets 1 possesion).
  • Difficulty: All Madden.
  • Qtr length: 6min.
  • Acceleration clock: off
  • Single elimination.
  • Playbooks: No Customs.

Circuit Admins

Circuit Admins control the flow of the tournament

Submit your match proof by following these steps...

  • Take a photo/screenshot of the final score at the end of the game.
  • Submit that photo to your circuit admin of the tournament.
  • Wait for confirmation.
  • If you have any disputes, please discuss everything with your circuit admin.
